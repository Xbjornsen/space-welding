About Endri

Hi, I'm Endri Prifti 36 years old, a fully qualified Boilermaker with a Certification III in Engineering Fabrication Trade and over 15 years of extensive experience in the industry. Specialise in metal fabrication, aluminum and stainless steel welding, we offer maintenance and repair services tailored to meet your unique needs. With a commitment to precision, durability, and exceptional craftsmanship, we take pride in our ability to handle projects of all sizes, delivering quality results that exceed expectations. Whether you need structural fabrication work, expert welding, routine maintenance, or urgent repair services, we are equipped to provide reliable and lasting solutions. Trust Space Welding and Fabrication to bring your ideas to reality with expertise and dedication you can count on. Let us get to work on enhancing and maintaining the integrity of your projects today!.

Services

Professional welding solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial needs

MIG & TIG Welding

Fast, efficient MIG welding for steel and aluminum projects, plus precision TIG welding for stainless steel, aluminum, and exotic metals. Perfect for high-quality welds on any project.

Flux Core & Stick Welding

Versatile flux-core and stick welding for outdoor and windy conditions. Ideal for heavy-duty structural work, repairs, and construction projects where portability and power are essential.

Custom Fabrication

Bring your ideas to life with custom metalwork. From concept to completion, I create bespoke solutions tailored to your needs.

Repairs & Maintenance

Quick and reliable repairs for damaged metal structures, machinery, and equipment. Get back to work faster with on-site service.

Railings & Gates

Beautiful and durable railings, gates, and fencing for residential and commercial properties. Custom designs available.

Mobile Welding

Full welding services at your location. Equipped for on-site work across Darwin and rural areas - no job too remote!
